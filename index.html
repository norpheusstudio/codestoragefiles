Explore TVL-ICT Strand

What is TVL-ICT Strand?

The TVL-ICT strand focuses on providing students with skills and knowledge in information and communication technology.

It covers topics such as computer programming, web development, networking, and digital media production.

Why Choose TVL-ICT?

TVL-ICT prepares students for various careers in the fast-growing technology industry.

It equips students with practical skills that are highly in demand in today's job market.

Explore Career Opportunities

With TVL-ICT, students can pursue careers as:

  • Software Developer
  • Web Designer
  • Network Administrator
  • Graphic Designer
  • IT Support Specialist
